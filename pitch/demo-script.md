# Truthly — Live Demo Script
**Total demo time: ~4 minutes**
**Browser tab pre-loaded:** http://localhost:5173 (elderly profile, Home page)

---

## ① Profile Switch (20 sec)

**Action:** Point to the Senior profile chip in the nav.

**Say:**
> "Truthly has two completely different modes — Senior and Kids. Same AI underneath, completely different experience. Watch."

**Action:** Click the profile chip to switch to Kids mode. Let the theme change register.

**Say:**
> "The language, the tone, the scam types — all change. Same protection, built for who you actually are."

**Action:** Switch back to Senior/Elderly.

---

## ② Detector (60 sec)

**Action:** Click "Detector" in the nav.

**Say:**
> "Let's say you've just received this text."

**Action:** Paste this message into the Detector input:

```
Dear Margaret, HMRC has identified an unpaid tax liability of £847.30 on your account.
Failure to resolve this within 24 hours will result in legal proceedings.
Please call 0800 085 3300 immediately or visit: hmrc-refund-portal.co.uk/verify
Reference: UK/TAX/2026/4471
```

**Action:** Click Analyse / Submit.

**Say (while result loads):**
> "We're not going to tell her this is a scam. We're going to show her the patterns and let her decide."

**Action:** When result appears, point to the severity band and flags.

**Say:**
> "High concern. Three flags — urgency pressure, fake domain, authority impersonation. Not a verdict. A map."

---

## ③ ScamTwin (75 sec)

**Action:** Click "Scam Twin" in the nav.

**Say:**
> "Knowing the patterns is one thing. Recognising them under pressure is another. ScamTwin lets you practice."

**Action:** Click to start a scenario. Choose "Realistic" difficulty.

**Say:**
> "An AI is now playing a scammer. I'm going to reply like someone who's a bit unsure."

**Action:** Type a vague reply like: "I'm not sure, can you tell me more?"

**Say (while AI responds):**
> "Watch how it adapts — adds more urgency, tries a different angle."

**Action:** Send one more reply: "This doesn't feel right."

**Say:**
> "There — the AI flags the tactics you spotted. You've just practiced something that could save you hundreds of pounds."

## ④ SafeTools (20 sec)

**Action:** Click "Trusted Tools" / "🔗 SafeTools" in the nav.

**Say:**

**Say:**
